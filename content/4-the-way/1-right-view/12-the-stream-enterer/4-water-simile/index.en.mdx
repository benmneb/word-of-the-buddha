There are these seven kinds of persons similar to those in the water:

1. One who goes under and drowns – meaning one with bad qualities
1. One who floats then drowns – meaning good at first but then bad qualities dominate
1. One who floats and keeps their head above water – meaning the good qualities become ever stronger
1. The above who looks around and sees safety – meaning the Stream Enterer
1. The above who is swimming to safety - meaning the Once Returner
1. The above who feels solid earth underfoot – meaning the Non-Returner
1. The above who is safe on dry land – meaning the Arahant [AN 7.15]
