[MN 22] When you contemplate in this way, three fetters are abandoned in you: a view of a permanent essence (soul), sceptical doubt, and belief that rites and rituals (are sufficient in themselves to reach enlightenment).Those who have abandoned three fetters are all stream-enterers, no longer subject to rebirth in a lower realm and headed for full enlightenment.

> [Dhp 178] Absolute rule over the earth, going to heaven, supreme sovereignty over all worlds—the fruit of stream winning surpasses them all.
