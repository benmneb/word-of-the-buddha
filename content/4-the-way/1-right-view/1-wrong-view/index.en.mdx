What, now, is wrong view?

\[DN 1, 2.13] There are some who state: “Whatever is called sight, hearing, smell, taste or touch; that is impermanent, unstable, non-eternal, and liable to change. But what is called ‘mentality’ (mano) or ‘mind’ (citta) or ‘consciousness’ (viññāna), that is the permanent essence, stable, eternal, not subject to change, the same forever and ever.” \[Brahmajala Sutta, Digha Nikaya, Wrong View No. 8 of 62 Wrong Views]
